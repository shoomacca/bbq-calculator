SECTION 03 // COMBUSTION

Wood Pairing Matrix

A guide to wood density, strength, and flavor profiles for smoking.

Wood Strength Profile Best Paired With
Oak Medium Clean, classic smoke flavor. The most versatile wood for long brisket cooks. Beef Brisket, Lamb, Pork, Sausages
Hickory Strong Stinging, heavy bacon flavor profile. Can turn bitter if used in excess. Pork Shoulders, Ribs, Red Meat
Mesquite Strong Very intense, earthy smoke. Burns down fast, perfect for hot & fast cooks. Steaks, Dark Red Meat
Apple Mild Subtle, sweet, and fruity. Requires a longer cook to fully penetrate meat. Pork, Poultry, Ribs
Cherry Mild Sweet, subtle fruit aroma. Lends a deep reddish tint to the meat's bark. Poultry, Pork Ribs, Beef
Pecan Medium Rich, nutty, and sweet. Excellent when blended with Oak for long smokes. Pork, Chicken, Beef
